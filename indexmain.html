How does the system work

In this present work, I have generalized the process of making the supermarket billing system as much automated as possible to make the tedious and cumbersome procedure of waiting in a queue after shopping a quick and intimidating experience. Armed only with smartphone app, smart card or palm, a customer could enter the store, choose which ever product they want from the aisles and refrigeration cases, and just complete a easy procedure of check out and walk out - no lines, no waiting, no cashier. All these operations are automated which is facilitated by sensor-based information technology, computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning. For these project, I have researched and studied the case of Amazon go by Amazon.com Inc (Amazon) and Hema stores by Alibaba.

The idea of auto billing and payment without a cashier have always captivated computer scientists and engineers for years and is having boom in every country. Every one is trying to achieve world full of robot and A.I dong all labor work. In 1937, grocer Clarence Saunder, founder of Piggly Wiggly grocery store chain laid the foundation of ’Keedoozle’, a revolutionary store. Keedoozle automatically dispensed groceries and created a bill. However, lurking behind of the store was a manual, labor-handled stocking and refilling system. With the growing demand, unfortunately, was not able to put up with. In a innovative store, the foremost requirement is to make the purchase within 5 to 10min to make purchase friction-free. The model of store will be 1) Check-in gates 2) Automated Carts 3) Aisles of product where every product has tags/codes 4) Check-out gates. The shop will be having cameras on top and shelves observing the customer’s selection. Check-out gate will have well functioned scanners which will scan product in the cart accurately and display on the screen. The mobile phone application will have a virtual shopping cart which will show your purchase summary after checkout. At checkout gate, the customer will be given options for the payment from which they can select one and checkout. This way might change the peoples’ usual shopping experience to a different level.
