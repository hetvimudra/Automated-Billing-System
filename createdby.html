In modern days, people are getting too busy in their works but still they need to spend time in shopping malls. In Shopping malls, customers face a difficulty to follow a queue for the process of billing, which takes a long time under huge crowd. Customer thought that, to avoid the standing time in the billing section at shopping malls. To overcome the above problems, we emerged the Automated Billing System In Shopping Mall system. To increase the pace of a billing process, RFID tags in products, then the product is read by the RFID scanner which describes the cost of that product and is displays on the LCD screen attached in our shopping list. As customer buy their exact product its price is add on the previous list. The cost of the product is displayed on the LCD screen by using microcontroller through Arduino Communication from host PC. Arduino supports bidirectional communication between microcontroller and host PC. We have proposed the system using robotics so e-product will be added in cart so cart will have no weight and easy to handle the items and it is directly stored in the server database and at that time of billing products will be avail to customer. Customer get direct bill at the billing section which is already stored at host PC which automatically reduces the queue.
